# About

**Sweats E-commerce** is a Persian-language online store and learning platform focused on homemade sweets and cakes.

The site combines:

- A product shop with cart, checkout, and wallet payments
- Educational content: blog posts, video tutorials, and structured courses
- Customer support via in-app messaging and a contact form

Built for the Iranian market: RTL layout, Toman pricing, Zarinpal payments, and Tehran timezone.

Technical details: see [README.md](README.md) and [docs/](docs/).
